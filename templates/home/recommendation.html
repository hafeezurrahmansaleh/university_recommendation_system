University recommendation

This system classifies the study area from the analysis of students' dataset related to the academic field. I have used the required dataset containing students' profiles and their application was accepted or rejected. Based on this information I have used the KNN algorithm for recommending top K universities from N similar users.

From this recommendation students will be able to enhance the possibility of getting admission.
